PrepDD Details

PreDD

A smart workspace for accountants, where you can manage all of your projects from the same place.

Tech Used:

React | RoR | PostgreSQL | Sidekiq | GraphQL | Apollo | Heroku

PrepDD is a smart workspace for accountants, where they can manage all of their projects at one place. It simplifies the file sharing process for teams and organizations. It helps in connecting the people, processes and information at one place. I have worked on PrepDD to develop Streamline Workflows that could assign, track and manage all of the team progress from the same place. Through the help of this platform, account reconciliations can easily be done due to the integration of the General Ledger software that ensures the accuracy of data. The dashboard of application also helps in the segregation of duties, documenting the sign-offs, and maintaining the document repositories.

This application contained a dashboard with reusable components, due to which I used React. For relational database, I used PostgreSQL and at its backend RoR is used. To achieve the Job Scheduling feature, Sidekiq was implemented. The application used GraphQL for data queries and Apollo to combine APIs, databases, and microservices into a single data graph. Application is being hosted at Heroku Cloud platform.

AirAsia Details

Air Asia

The Asean super app for booking flights, hotels, activities, food, unlimited deals and so much more!

Tech Used:

React | RoR | MySQL | CircleCI | TDD | Jira

AirAsia is a Malaysian airline headquartered near Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. It is the largest airline in Malaysia by fleet size and destinations. I worked as a remote ruby on rails developer in activities and packages department of AirAsia. The purpose of this department (https://www.airasia.com/activities/en/gb) is to maintain APIs that provides white-label web app solution including activity booking and video-based travel discovery features. My responsibilities include the test-driven development (TDD), writing new APIs and bug fixing. Being a full-stack RoR developer, I was responsible for handling both frontend and backend of the website.

Due to its good ecosystem, React is used at the front-end of Activities section, while RoR is used at the backend of website. Since this app was using user’s data that could provide them customized packages and details, MySQL is used for Database Management. To provide continuous integration and delivery CircleCI is employed.

RadianGen

A solar energy asset manager focused on the commercial and utility-scale PV sector.

Tech Used:

Ruby on Rails | Angular | MySQL | Redis | Rest APIs | Sidekiq

Radian Generation is one of the five largest independent solar asset management company worldwide. Of various platforms of Radian, Lens is one of them. The Lens Platform is simply developed to automate the mundane and highlight the exceptional. It provides superior efficiencies, redundancies and results. Settle Details

Settle Finance

A deeply interconnected Decentralized Applications (DApps) integrated with Decentralized Finance (DeFi)

Tech Used:

Node.js | React | RethinkDB | Socket.io | Redux

Settle is an open community of blockchain builders, enthusiasts and researchers working towards a free, bountiful and decentralized future for all humans. DEX.AG Details

DEX.AG

An aggregator that gets you the best price for your trade across multiple DEXes.

Tech Used:

Node.js | Vue.js | MongoDB | Socket.IO

DEX.AG provides a platform integration solution with their APIs and SDK to the trading companies, by removing the complete hazzle of maintaining the backend code. It helps the companies to build the systems with smarter not harder approach.

Since the trading involves real-time data, that changes every second, and traders had to respond immediately with the changing trends. Therefore, to make the systems less prone to errors, I used Vue.js, a JavaScript Framework that is written in TypeScript, for frontend development. While I used Node.js for the development of backend. For the client-server communication, Socket.IO was used. For the NoSQL database program, I used MongoDB that helped me in creating cross-platform document-oriented database.

Whisp Details

WhispMoney

A decentralized Payroll for remote workers — easier, faster, cheaper.

SimplyMadeLocal Details

Simply Made Local

MakeAnOffer Details

MakeAnOffer

Never lose a customer because of price

Tech Used:

React | Ruby on Rails | PostgreSQL | Shopify
